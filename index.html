Native Polish flora

Spergula morisonni, commonly known as Morison's spurry, lives on sandy grasslands, fallow fields and forest edges

Ajuga reptans is known as blue bugle. It prefers sunny and semi-shaded positions, although it does well in the shade. It grows in deciduous forests, on existing soils, rich in humus and mineral residues. In the mountains he escapes to the upper montane.

Neottia nidus-avis known as the bird's-nest orchid, is a non-photosynthetic orchid, native to Europe. A perennial plant that is hidden underground for most of the year. platforms during the period of sprouting above-ground shoots, which die after releasing seeds. It is a myco-heterotroph that poses a threat to the growth it derives from the fungi it parasitizes. Seeds can only germinate after they have penetrated their central fungal hypha.

Melittis melissophyllum is a species of flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae. Its common name is bastard balm. Perennial, hemicryptophyte. It blooms from May to July. Scattered in lowlands and lower mountainous locations, rare. It grows in bright deciduous forests and thickets.
